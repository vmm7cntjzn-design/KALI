Die Reise

Embark on a captivating 10-day journey through Kenya's diverse landscapes and cultural treasures. The adventure begins in Nairobi, leading to Sagana, where adrenaline awaits with activities like ziplining, kayaking, and rafting at the Getway Resort. The expedition then heads to Meru National Park, known for its untouched beauty and abundant wildlife, offering a unique safari experience at Ikweta Safari Camp. Continue to the Samburu Game Reserve for encounters with Grevy's zebras, elephants, and diverse bird species, finding comfort and hospitality at Samburu Intrepids. Explore the scenic Ngare Ndare Forest, with its captivating canopy walkway and waterfalls, before heading.  Then travel to the Laikipia region, where you have the chance to see rare animals like black leopards and wild dogs. The journey proceeds to Ol Pejeta Conservancy, a haven for wildlife conservation and home to the last northern white rhinos, with accommodation at Serena Sweetwaters. The final day brings a game drive through Ol Pejeta and a return to Nairobi, concluding this remarkable expedition with memories of Kenya's natural wonders and vibrant cultures.
