10 Days Maasai Wedding and Honeymoon

📅 10 Tage 💰 ab 2.059 USD pp 🇰🇪 Kenia
Überblick

Die Reise

Embark on a unique and romantic 10-day journey through Kenya, beginning with a city stay at Karen Blixen Coffee Garden in Nairobi. Your adventure truly commences with a flight to the Maasai Mara Game Reserve, a wildlife haven renowned for its annual wildebeest migration. Nestled in the heart of this breathtaking landscape is Leopard Hill, your luxurious accommodation, offering splendid views of the Naboisho Conservancy. Immerse yourself in the wonders of the Maasai Mara, with opportunities for hot air balloon rides and a chance to experience a traditional Maasai wedding ceremony. Your journey continues as you fly to Diani Beach, where Chale Island awaits. Here, you'll reside in an Overwater Suite or On the Rock Suite at The Sands at Chale Island, surrounded by indigenous forest and pristine beaches. Enjoy a range of activities, from snorkeling to spa treatments, before concluding your idyllic honeymoon with a drop-off at Mombasa Airport for your international departure. This itinerary promises a perfect blend of wildlife adventure, cultural experiences, and romantic beachside relaxation.
