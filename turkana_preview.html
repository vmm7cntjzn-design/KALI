07 Days Lake Turkana Adventure

Überblick

Die Reise

Embark on a captivating 7-day adventure around Lake Turkana, starting with a morning flight from Nairobi to Lodwar. Upon arrival, you'll be greeted by a representative and driven to Eliye Springs on the shores of Lake Turkana, the world's largest desert lake known as the cradle of mankind. Stay at the Eliye Springs Resort, experiencing traditional Turkana culture and visiting the fishing village of Longelech. Explore Nariokotome, home to the Turkana boy fossil site, and visit Kalokol, a center for Turkana fishermen. Venture to Central Island National Park with its volcanic crater lakes and Nile Crocodile breeding grounds. Relax on the beach or opt for a bird watching tour on Central Island. Head to Moite to discover the El Molo ethnic group, exclusive traditional fishermen, and visit their sacred shrine. Spend your last day enjoying the beach, sun, and optional activities like a massage or fishing trip. Conclude your unforgettable journey with a boat trip and Sundowner before departing from Lodwar to Nairobi on day 7.
